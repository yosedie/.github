# Support Policy

Thank you for using our projects! If you need help, have questions, or want to discuss something, please refer to the resources listed below.

## How to Get Support

### 1. GitHub Discussions / Issues
For public questions, troubleshooting, or general usage discussions:
*   **Ask a Question:** If the repository has *GitHub Discussions* enabled, please post your question there.
*   **Open an Issue:** If you suspect you've found a bug or have a specific feature request, please check existing issues first and then open a new issue.

### 2. Contacting the Author
For private inquiries, business proposals, or questions that cannot be discussed publicly:
*   **Email:** You can reach out directly via **yose.die.business@gmail.com**.
*   **Website/Portfolio:** Visit [yosedie.github.io](https://yosedie.github.io) for more contact options and social links.

## Response Time

Please keep in mind that many of these projects are open-source and maintained in our free time. We do our best to respond as quickly as possible, usually within a few days. Thank you for your patience and understanding!
